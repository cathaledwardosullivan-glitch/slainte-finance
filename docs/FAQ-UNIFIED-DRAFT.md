## About Sláinte Finance

### What is Sláinte Finance?

Sláinte Finance is an AI-powered financial management platform built specifically for Irish general practice. It helps GPs and practice managers take control of their finances by automating bank transaction categorisation, generating professional reports, reconciling GMS and HSE payments, analysing financial trends, and providing on-demand financial insights through Finn, our built-in AI Financial Advisor. The platform was designed by a practising GP who understands the day-to-day financial challenges of running a surgery.

### Who is Sláinte Finance designed for?

Sláinte Finance is designed for Irish GP practices of all sizes, from single-handed practices to multi-partner surgeries. It serves GPs who want visibility over their financial position, practice managers who handle day-to-day finances, and administrative staff involved in bookkeeping and payment reconciliation. You do not need any accounting expertise to use it.

### What makes Sláinte Finance different from general accounting software like Sage or Xero?

Unlike generic accounting tools, Sláinte Finance is purpose-built for the Irish general practice environment. It comes pre-configured with over 90 GP-specific expense and income categories, including GMS payments, HSE contracts, medical indemnity, locum costs, vaccine supplies, medical council subscriptions, and many more.

It understands Irish GP practice workflows, handles Irish date formats, and generates reports tailored to what your accountant actually needs at year-end. General accounting software requires significant manual configuration to achieve anything close to this level of relevance.

### Was Sláinte Finance built by people who understand general practice?

Yes. Sláinte Finance was co-founded by a practising GP in Dublin who spent over a decade managing practice finances and experienced first-hand the limitations of spreadsheets and generic accounting tools. The platform was born from that real-world experience, which is why it addresses the specific pain points that GPs and practice managers actually face.

## Features & Functionality

### What are the core features of Sláinte Finance?

Sláinte Finance offers a comprehensive suite of financial management tools: a real-time financial dashboard showing your practice's financial position at a glance; automatic bank transaction categorisation using AI that learns your patterns over time; GMS and HSE payment reconciliation to match incoming payments against expected amounts; financial trend analysis to help with planning decisions; professional report generation for partners' meetings, accountants, and year-end; year-over-year comparison of income, expenses, and profit; and Finn, your AI Financial Advisor, for on-demand financial analysis and strategic planning.

The platform is designed to replace the manual spreadsheet work that most practices currently rely on, saving significant administrative time each month.

### How does automatic transaction categorisation work?

When you upload your bank statement (CSV file or bank statement PDF), Sláinte Finance automatically categorises each transaction using a combination of pre-built pattern matching and AI learning. The platform recognises over 90 GP-specific categories such as GMS payments, vaccine suppliers like Sanofi and GSK, medical suppliers like Fitzmedical and United Drug, utility providers, insurance, and professional subscriptions.

For transactions it cannot categorise automatically, you simply assign them manually once. The AI remembers your choice and will categorise identical transactions automatically in future. The system gets smarter the more you use it.

### What is Finn, the AI Financial Advisor?

Finn is your dedicated, built-in AI Financial Advisor. You can ask Finn complex financial questions in plain English and receive immediate analysis based on your actual practice data.

For example, you could ask: "Has the €800 advertising campaign last month paid off in travel vaccine uptake?" Finn might respond: "The €800 spend in April saw a rise in Travel Medicine income from €1,200 to €1,500 year-on-year this month. While a net return isn't yet visible, we recommend waiting three more months for the true impact."

You can also use Finn for strategic planning. Ask something like: "What would be the impact on the business if we offered a partnership to our salaried GP?" and Finn will generate a detailed report exploring the potential financial implications, including revenue considerations, profit-sharing scenarios, and longer-term impacts on the practice.

Finn bases its analysis on your real financial data, so its insights are grounded in actual numbers — but it is a decision-support tool, not a replacement for professional financial advice. For significant decisions, always consult your accountant.

### Can Sláinte Finance handle GMS payment reconciliation?

Yes. GMS payment reconciliation is one of the platform's core features. It automatically matches HSE and GMS payments against expected amounts, flags discrepancies, and significantly reduces the administrative burden of manually checking that you have been paid correctly. This is one of the most time-consuming financial tasks in any practice, and automating it can save hours each month.

### What reports can Sláinte Finance generate?

Sláinte Finance generates a range of professional reports designed for the needs of Irish GP practices. These include a full profit and loss statement, income summaries broken down by category (GMS, private fees, state contracts, insurance, etc.), detailed expenditure breakdowns (salaries, building costs, medical supplies, subscriptions, etc.), partner drawings summaries, year-over-year comparisons with percentage changes, personal tax return templates to help organise your Form 11 figures, and a complete labelled transaction listing.

Reports can be exported in formats that are easy to share with your accountant at year-end or present at partners' meetings.

### How do I get my bank data into Sláinte Finance?

You have two options:

1. **CSV files** — Download your bank statements as a CSV file from your online banking portal, then upload them directly into Sláinte Finance using the simple drag-and-drop upload feature. The platform handles Irish date formats (DD/MM/YYYY) automatically. Most Irish banks offer CSV download as a standard feature of their business banking portals.

2. **Bank statement PDFs** — You can also upload your bank statement PDFs directly. Sláinte Finance includes a built-in PDF parser that extracts transactions automatically. Bank of Ireland statements are currently fully supported, with additional banks being added based on user feedback.

### Does the platform support multiple years of data for comparison?

Yes. Sláinte Finance supports year-over-year analysis, allowing you to compare income, expenses, and profit across financial years. This is invaluable for identifying trends, assessing growth, and preparing for accountant reviews or partners' meetings.

## Data Security & Privacy

### Is our financial data safe with Sláinte Finance?

Yes. Sláinte Finance is installed directly on your practice's own computer. Your financial data is stored locally and is never uploaded to our servers or stored externally.

When you use AI-powered features like Finn, summarised financial data is sent securely to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. This uses the same level of encryption as online banking (TLS), and Anthropic does not store or use your data for model training. Sensitive details — bank account numbers, sort codes, patient data, and PCRS credentials — are never included in AI queries under any circumstances.

If you prefer no external connections at all, you can enable Local Only Mode from Settings, which disables all AI features and ensures nothing leaves your device. See our [Privacy Policy](https://slainte.app/privacy-policy) for full details.

### Can anyone else see our financial information?

Your raw financial data is stored on your computer and is not accessible to anyone at Sláinte Finance or any third party. We cannot see, retrieve, or access your financial records.

When you use AI features, financial summaries (not raw data) are sent securely to Anthropic for processing. Anthropic processes the data to generate a response and does not retain it. Our [Privacy Policy](https://slainte.app/privacy-policy) details exactly what data is and isn't included in AI queries. If you enable Local Only Mode, no data leaves your device at all.

### What happens if Sláinte Finance gets hacked?

Because your financial data is stored locally on your own computer and not on any external server, a breach of Sláinte Finance's corporate systems would not expose your practice's financial data. Your data is not held centrally. The risk profile is fundamentally different from cloud-based SaaS products, where a single breach can expose thousands of customers' data simultaneously.

The only data we hold externally is basic practice registration information (a randomly generated Practice ID and your practice name) used for support purposes. No financial data is stored on our systems.

### Where exactly is the data stored?

Your financial data is stored on the computer where Sláinte Finance is installed, within your practice's own infrastructure. It is not uploaded to the cloud, replicated to external servers, or stored externally.

The only external data transmission occurs when you use AI-powered features (financial summaries sent securely to Anthropic) or when you submit feedback (sent to our support system). You can disable all external connections by enabling Local Only Mode in Settings. See our [Privacy Policy](https://slainte.app/privacy-policy) for a detailed breakdown of what data goes where.

### What makes storing data locally more secure than cloud-based alternatives?

Sláinte Finance's local-first architecture ensures that your financial data stays within your own infrastructure. This eliminates the risks associated with cloud storage, including centralised data breaches, unauthorised access by third parties, and the complexities of cross-border data transfer. For practices handling sensitive financial data, keeping it on your own hardware gives you direct control over security.

When you use AI features, only the minimum necessary financial summaries are sent externally, and these are processed in real-time without being stored. In Local Only Mode, no data leaves your device under any circumstances.

### Can Sláinte Finance work without an internet connection?

Yes. In Local Only Mode, the platform is fully functional without any internet connection. Your day-to-day transaction management, rule-based categorisation, reporting, GMS Health Check analysis, encrypted backups, and financial dashboards all work entirely offline.

Features that rely on AI processing — Finn's chat and analysis, AI-assisted categorisation, and automated PCRS statement downloads — require an internet connection. You can switch between modes at any time from Settings.

### Is Sláinte Finance GDPR compliant?

Yes. Sláinte Finance is designed with GDPR compliance in mind:

- **Your financial data** remains on your local machine and is never stored on our servers.
- **AI features** use Anthropic as a data processor. Anthropic processes your queries securely and does not retain or use your data for training. Our [Privacy Policy](https://slainte.app/privacy-policy) details the lawful basis for this processing.
- **Practice registration data** (Practice ID and practice name) is stored in Google Workspace for support purposes. This is disclosed in our Privacy Policy and covered by your consent during setup.
- **Local Only Mode** eliminates all external data processing for practices that require complete data isolation.

You can exercise your GDPR rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability) at any time by contacting us. Full details are in our [Privacy Policy](https://slainte.app/privacy-policy).

## Technology & AI

### Sláinte Finance uses AI — what if the AI provider goes out of business?

The platform's AI capabilities are currently powered by Claude, one of the world's leading AI models. However, the architecture is designed to be AI-provider agnostic. If a different AI model becomes the market leader, or if the current provider were to become unavailable, we can switch to an alternative AI engine without disrupting your service. You would not need to change anything on your end.

### Does the AI learn from my practice's data?

The AI learns your transaction categorisation patterns locally — for example, if you categorise a payment from a specific supplier once, it will remember that for future transactions. However, your data is never sent to any AI company for model training purposes. Your financial information is not used to improve any external AI model.

### How accurate is the AI transaction categorisation?

The categorisation accuracy improves over time as the system learns your specific patterns. On initial setup, the pre-built pattern matching handles the majority of common GP practice transactions (GMS payments, well-known suppliers, utility providers, etc.) accurately. For less common or practice-specific transactions, you categorise them once and the AI handles them automatically thereafter. Most practices find that after the first one to two months of use, the vast majority of transactions are categorised correctly without any manual intervention.

### Can I trust the financial advice Finn provides?

Finn bases its analysis on your actual financial data, so its insights are grounded in real numbers. However, Finn is a decision-support tool, not a replacement for professional financial or accounting advice. We recommend using Finn for day-to-day financial visibility, trend analysis, and preliminary strategic planning, and continuing to work with your accountant for tax, compliance, and formal financial reporting.

### What if the AI makes a mistake in categorising a transaction?

You can easily correct any miscategorised transaction with a single click. Once corrected, the AI will learn from your correction and apply it to similar transactions in future. The platform is designed with the understanding that human oversight is essential, and every automated decision can be reviewed and amended.

## Getting Started

### How long does it take to set up Sláinte Finance?

Installation takes just a few minutes — it's a standard desktop application. The full onboarding process, including setting up your practice profile, uploading your first bank statements, and reviewing the initial categorisation, typically takes around 30 minutes. The AI-guided setup walks you through each step.

### What equipment or system requirements do I need?

Sláinte Finance runs on a standard Windows 10 or Windows 11 computer. There are no special hardware requirements — the same computer your practice already uses for day-to-day administration is sufficient. No server infrastructure is needed.

### Do I need any accounting or technical expertise to use it?

No. Sláinte Finance is designed for GPs and practice managers, not accountants or IT specialists. The interface is intuitive, and the platform handles the complexity behind the scenes. If you can download a bank statement and open an app, you can use Sláinte Finance.

### How do I get value from the platform quickly?

Most practices see value within the first week. Upload your current year's bank statements, let the AI categorise your transactions, review and correct any that need adjustment, and you will have a clear financial picture of your practice almost immediately. From there, the platform only gets more useful as it learns your patterns.

### Can multiple people in the practice use it?

Yes. The desktop application can be used by anyone with access to the practice computer where it is installed. Additionally, practice partners and staff can view the financial dashboard from their own phone or tablet over your practice's local network, protected by an access password you set during setup. This means your practice manager can handle day-to-day transaction management on the main computer while partners can check dashboards and reports from their own devices.

### What training and support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive onboarding support, including guided setup, initial training for all users, and ongoing access to our support team. During the beta period, practices also benefit from direct access to the founding team, including regular check-ins and priority support.

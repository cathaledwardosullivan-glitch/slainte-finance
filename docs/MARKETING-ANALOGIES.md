# Sláinte Finance — Marketing Analogies

---

## For GPs

### One-liner
> Sláinte Finance turns your bank statements into a radiology report — everything interpreted, flagged, and ready for your decision.

### Short (website / pitch deck)

**From 100 CT slices to a full 3D reconstruction — with a report.**

Your bank statements are raw data. Hundreds of lines, thousands of euros, and somewhere in there is the diagnosis. Sláinte Finance reconstructs the full picture and hands you the report: what's normal, what's changed, what needs your attention. Want to interrogate a finding? Just ask Finn — like having the radiologist on the phone, walking you through it in plain English.

### Full (blog / brochure / long-form)

**It's the difference between 100 CT slices and a 3D reconstruction with a report.**

Your bank statements are like raw CT data. Hundreds of lines, thousands of euros moving in and out, and somewhere in there is the diagnosis — but you'd need hours to find it yourself. Sláinte Finance takes those slices, reconstructs the full picture, and hands you the report: here's what's normal, here's what's changed, here's what needs your attention. And if you want to interrogate a finding further, you just ask Finn — like having the radiologist on the phone, walking you through the images in plain English.

## For Practice Managers

### One-liner
> Sláinte Finance is like an assistant who's also a qualified accountant — your figures sorted, reconciled, and waiting for your approval every month.

### Short (website / pitch deck)

**Like hiring an assistant who happens to be a qualified accountant.**

Every month, your bank statements are pulled, every transaction categorised, your GMS payments reconciled against what the HSE actually paid — and the whole thing presented to you in a neat summary, ready for your approval. Anything that doesn't look right is already circled. You skip the sorting and go straight to the decisions.

No sick days. No desk required. Ready when you are — even at 9pm on a Sunday.

### Full (blog / brochure / long-form)

**It's like hiring an assistant who happens to be a qualified accountant.**

Imagine you had someone who came in every month, pulled your bank statements, sorted every transaction into the right category, reconciled your GMS payments against what the HSE actually paid, and then presented the whole thing to you in a neat summary — ready for your approval. They don't make decisions for you. They don't file your tax return. But by the time you sit down to review the figures, the work is done. And if something doesn't look right — a payment that's short, an expense that's crept up — they've already circled it.

That's Sláinte Finance. Except it doesn't take sick days, it doesn't need a desk, and it's ready whenever you are — even at 9pm on a Sunday when you're trying to get ahead of the week.

## 2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE

Sláinte Finance is a desktop application designed to help Irish GP practices manage and analyse their financial data, including:
- Bank statement categorisation and analysis
- PCRS/GMS payment tracking and analysis
- AI-powered financial insights via Finn (powered by Anthropic's Claude)
- Practice health check metrics

## 3. DATA STORAGE AND PRIVACY

### 3.1 Local Storage
All financial data you enter or import is stored locally on your device. The App does not transmit your financial data to external servers for storage.

### 3.2 AI Features
When you use AI-powered features (such as Finn, the financial assistant), the following data may be sent to Anthropic's Claude API over encrypted connections:
- Your questions and conversation history
- Aggregated financial summaries (totals, categories, monthly trends)
- Practice name and staff/GP names
- GMS payment amounts
- Transaction descriptions for uncategorised items (approximately 5% of transactions)
- For detailed reports: complete financial breakdowns
- During onboarding: your practice website content (if you choose website analysis)

This data is processed by Anthropic solely to generate responses and is not used to train AI models, in accordance with Anthropic's API terms.

### 3.3 What is Never Sent
The following data never leaves your device under any circumstances:
- Bank account numbers, sort codes, or IBANs
- Individual patient data
- PCRS portal login credentials
- Your Sláinte Key (used only for authentication)
- Raw bank statement files

### 3.4 PCRS Integration
When using the PCRS download feature:
- Your PCRS portal credentials are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage
- Session cookies are encrypted and stored locally
- We do not have access to your PCRS login credentials

### 3.5 Local Only Mode
You may enable "Local Only Mode" at any time from Settings. When enabled:
- No external connections are made from the App
- Finn chat, AI categorisation assistance, website analysis, and PCRS automated downloads are disabled
- All other features continue to work, including rule-based transaction categorisation, financial calculations, GMS Health Check analysis, and encrypted backups
- You can switch back to AI-powered mode at any time from Settings

### 3.6 No Third-Party Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Anthropic is our sole data sub-processor, used exclusively for AI-powered features.

### 3.7 No Tracking or Telemetry
The App contains no analytics, usage tracking, advertising, or telemetry of any kind.
